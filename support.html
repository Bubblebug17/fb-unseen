How would you like to support FB unseen?
  
pollcode.com free polls 

TL;DR I’m interested in how many people would support me and what you are thinking of ads embedded on facebook, thats why I set up this poll.

Thank you that you want to support me. I‘m very happy to see that there are so much people enjoying my extension and that there are even people willing to support it. As the userbase is growing, I admit that I‘m starting to think about things like: “Wow, over 100 000 people installed your extension, just imagine half of them would give you $0.50!”

Although I have currently no financial problems, I’m just a student with a modest life, and therefore it is very alluring to insert ads on facebook to be able to buy or do things I would usually not do or need to save money for a long time. On the other hand, I would be not amused if I’m using some extension and from one day to another it would start showing me ads, even if I could disable them.

Recently I got another idea: What if I would create an amazon wishlist and put a link to it here? It would be kinda cool if from time to time I would receive packages containing little surprises, like some movies or books. And, well, I would also put insanely expensive things on the list, like a new notebook or stuff like that, although I would not expect that it would ever arrive. But imagine my face if you really gifted me things like that, how awesome would that be :D

I guess that this idea would not really work out, since most of the users are just installing the extension and then do nothing more with it. Just to give you some statistics I aggregatet with Google Analytics: While there are about 6.5 million pageviews of the background page in one month (effectively the number of times chrome with installed FB unseen is beeing started up), there are just 300 000 views of the popup to quickly dis- and enable it, and just 10 000 times the settings page is viewed! The majority seems to just install and then forget it. I’m really wondering how many people will participate in this poll. Just in case you are as curious as me and interested in further statistics, I have packed some Google Analytics reports in this zipfile for download.

Just in case I see no point in the „Amazon wishlist idea“ and decide to insert ads to facebook: I will definitely add an option to the settings page for people like you where you may disable the ads. Thank you for taking your time reading this.
